# 43 - Anti-Cheat Architecture

## Vision
Anti-cheat is a core design pillar, not a feature added after release. The goal is to protect fair competition and the integrity of the in-game economy.

## Design Principles
- Server-authoritative validation.
- Minimize client trust.
- Layered detection.
- Rapid response to abuse.
- Continuous monitoring.

## Detection Layers
- Impossible movement validation.
- Inventory consistency checks.
- Economy anomaly detection.
- Combat telemetry analysis.
- Automated replay review.

## Response Strategy
- Flag suspicious activity.
- Increase monitoring confidence.
- Restrict affected accounts when appropriate.
- Preserve evidence for investigation.

## Long-Term Goals
Reduce cheating incentives while maintaining a positive experience for legitimate players and avoiding unnecessary false positives.
