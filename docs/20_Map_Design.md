# 20 - Map Design

## Philosophy
Maps should naturally create encounters without forcing players together.

## Principles
- Multiple routes between objectives.
- Vertical gameplay.
- Distinct landmarks visible from long distance.
- Safe, contested and dangerous regions.
- Environmental storytelling.
- Hidden shortcuts and secrets.

## Points of Interest
Every POI should answer:
- Why would players come here?
- What risks exist?
- What unique gameplay happens here?
- What makes this location memorable?

No location should exist only to contain loot.
