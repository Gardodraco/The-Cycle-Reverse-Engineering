# 38 - Resource Economy

## Purpose
The resource economy is the backbone of progression. Every gathered item should have at least one meaningful use.

## Design Goals
- Avoid useless loot.
- Multiple crafting paths.
- Regional scarcity.
- Dynamic prices.
- Long-term demand.

## Resource Categories
- Metals
- Electronics
- Chemicals
- Organic materials
- Alien artifacts
- Energy components

## Principles
Resources should circulate continuously between exploration, crafting, quests, hideout upgrades and trading. Players should always face interesting choices between selling, crafting or keeping valuable materials.
