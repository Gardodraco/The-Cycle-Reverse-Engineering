# 39 - Crafting Architecture

## Vision
Crafting should reinforce exploration rather than replace it. Players are encouraged to venture into dangerous areas to obtain rare components instead of farming safe locations.

## Core Principles
- Every crafted item has a purpose.
- Multiple recipes may exist for similar outcomes.
- Rare components come from high-risk zones.
- Crafting times encourage planning without excessive waiting.

## Crafting Categories
- Weapons
- Attachments
- Medical items
- Ammunition
- Hideout modules
- Consumables
- Quest items

## Balancing
The crafting economy should continuously remove resources from circulation, supporting a healthy in-game economy while giving value to exploration and trading.
