# Project Vision

## Goal
Create a next-generation PvPvE extraction shooter inspired by the strengths of The Cycle: Frontier without copying its world, lore, assets, mechanics, or balance.

## Design Pillars
- Emergent gameplay
- Meaningful risk versus reward
- Living ecosystem
- Player-driven economy
- Long-term progression
- High replayability

This document is the starting point of a comprehensive design bible that will expand into detailed gameplay, technical, AI, economy, and world design documents.
